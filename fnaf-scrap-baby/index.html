FNAF Scrap Baby

“It feels like… my birthday. Did you have a gift for me?”

Scrap baby comes with three items:

  • One hair
  • One tiara (found in earrings)
  • One full body piece

Scrap baby lacks one of her hands as it is replaced with a claw, this does not affect her abilities, though she may hold some items weirdly. Likewise, the extended fingers on her other hand may clip through the hand itself.

To prevent weird floating jewelry, it’s best to remove rings and bracelets from her claw arm.

If you’d like to download the skin that Lauren uses, head over to The Sims Resource.
