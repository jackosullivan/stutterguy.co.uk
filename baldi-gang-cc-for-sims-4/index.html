Baldi Gang CC for Sims 4

“Wow! That’s great! Let’s play again, sometime soon.”

This bundle contains five items for four characters.

Playtime

  • A full body outfit
  • Scribble eyes

Whilst I wanted to make Playtime a 2D character, it felt like I was just stealing artwork from Mystman12 instead of creating my own resources. Also, I tried several methods for recreating the hair which either didn’t work or looked atrocious, so the hair and the shoes used in the preview image are from expansion packs.

Gotta Sweep

Gotta Sweep is a single item found in the full body section for adults. Be sure to download the No Teeth mod to remove his teeth.

1st Prize

1st Prize is a single item found in the full body section for kids. I wasn’t able to rig him so that he just glided along the floor smoothly, so we can pretend one of his wires has been cut. Be sure to download the No Teeth mod for kids to remove his teeth (they’re hid inside his head, but sometimes they pop out).

Arts and Crafters

Arts and Crafters is a single item found in the full body section for adults.

“Where’s Principal of the Thing and It’s a Bully?”

Principle of the Thing can be made using items that are already in the game, as can It’s a Bully. This little project only started to make a dress for Playtime as nothing similar existed in the game. Again, the reason I decided not to make Principal or the Thing is because I didn’t want to make a 2D character that is just stolen artwork.
