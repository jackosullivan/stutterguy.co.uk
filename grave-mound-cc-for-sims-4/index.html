Grave Mound CC for Sims 4

Rest in pizza.

This item contains a single object, which contains 20 swatches to match the base game terrain paints (except the masonry ones). Sims can walk around the outskirts of the mound, but will not walk into the middle.

The mound can be engraved, similar to a tombstone, but does not contain any ghosts. It is best used with tombstone to show where the coffin in buried.
