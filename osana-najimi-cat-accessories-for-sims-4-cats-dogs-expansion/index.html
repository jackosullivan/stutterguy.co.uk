Osana Najimi Cat Accessories for Sims 4 Cats & Dogs Expansion

This is just a quick little accessory that I made for Veggie Gamer to make a cat version of Osana Najimi.

The accessories are one item and can be found in cat clothing. Some sculpting of the ears and the cranium may be necessary to prevent the items clipping into the head. The items may not work with all the different fur thickness. You cannot use this item without the Cats and Dogs expansion.

The cat shown in the preview image is available on the Sims 4 gallery with the hashtag #StutterGuy. Be sure to allow CC results in your search.
